Dee Doke Waterfall

Dee Dote waterfall is a hidden gem located in the mountains of Mandalay city, Myanmar. Dee Dote is actually a three-tiered cascade with a series of falls and pools filled with clear water that flows from the Shan Plateau. A footpath leads down to the lowest, most spectacular pool, which dazzles with its blue lagoon pool and wonderful turquoise water. It is very beautiful waterfall. Visitors can relax and enjoy the natural beauty of waterfall.
