Myanmar

Myanmar, also called Burma, country, located in the western portion of mainland Southeast Asia. Stretching from latitude 10° N to about 28° 30' N, Myanmar is the northernmost country of Southeast Asia.The country is bordered by China to the north and northeast, Laos to the east, Thailand to the southeast, the Andaman Sea and Bay of Bengal to the south and southwest, Bangladesh to the west, and India to the northwest.

Cities

Myanmar is officially known as the Republic of the Union of Myanmar. The coastal region is known as Lower Myanmar, while the interior region is known as Upper Myanmar. The total area of the country is 676,578 square km (261,227 square miles). There are seven states and seven cities in Myanmar. Among them, Nay Pyi Daw is the capital city of Myanmar.Moreover,Yangon is the economical city and Mandalay is the last kingdom of Myanmar.

Nay Pyi Daw
Nay Pyi Daw, the capital city of Myanmar, is located between the two mountain ranges of the Bago Yoma and the Shan Yoma.Naypyidaw is a highly unique urban center in this region and the fully constructed city is quite large.Healthy and education infrastructure in Naypyidaw is yet to develop. And there are many interesting places are in there. Naypyidaw Safari Park and the Gem Museum in the city are also places worthy a visit as well. The Uppatasanti Pagoda or Peace Pagoda is the most prominent landmark pagoda in the city.
Yangon
Yangon was Myanmar’s administrative capital before Nay Pyi Taw was named as the capital city.In the ancient time,Yangon was called Rangoon.Yangon is located in the southern part of the country on the east bank of the Yangon, or Hlaing River. Yangon is the largest city in Myanmar and the industrial and commercial centre of the country. A visit to Yangon is incomplete without visiting Shwedagon pagoda.Shwe Dagon Pagoda is a great Buddhist temple complex.Yangon is the site of several other major religious edifices, including the Shwedagon pagoda and the Sule and Botataung pagodas.The famous museums are the Bogyoke Aung San Museum and the National Museum of Art and Archaeology.
Mandalay
Mandalay is the second-largest city of Myanmar, located on the east bank of Ayeyarwady river. It is regarded as a cultural centre of the country. Mandalay was the former capital of the country and has a rich history behind it. Mandalay was the last royal city before the country was colonised by Britain in 1885. Tourists can also visit the Mandalay Palace which was the last palace built by Burmese royals. The palace is located at the heart of Mandalay and is surrounded by a moat and a wall.

Climate

In Myanmar, there are three seasons.They are summer season, rainy season and winter season.Although Myanmar is located in the monsoon region of Asia, its climate is greatly modified by its geographic position and its relief.

Summer

The summer, dry intermonsoonal season start mid-February and end mid-May.

Rainy

The rainy season begin mid-May and end late October.

Winter

The winter season start late October and end mid-February.
