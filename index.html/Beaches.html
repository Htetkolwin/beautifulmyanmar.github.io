Ngapali Beach

Ngapali beach is the most beautiful beach in Myanmar. It is located a few kilometers from the town of Thandwe in Rakhin State, Western Myanmar. Ngapali is a great place to enjoy swimming in the warm and clear water or sunbathing while watching local fishermen bring their catch to shore.

Snorkeling

Sunset of Ngapali Beach

Visitors can relax on beach chairs, shade is provided by reed parasols and palm trees. And they can enjoy dinner on the beach while watching the beautiful sunset over the Bay of Bengal and taste the fresh seafoods. Besides sunbathing and swimming, visitors can go snorkeling to see the underwater world with lots of tropical colourful fishes. Trips to nearby small islands can be booked at hotels.
